# Life Dashboard

A personal productivity and wellness tracking application to help you organize your daily life, build habits, and maintain mindfulness.

## Features

- **Overview Dashboard** - Get a quick snapshot of your progress, streaks, and daily inspiration
- **Task Management** - Track and complete your to-do items
- **Habit Tracking** - Build consistency with daily habit completion
- **Journal** - Record your daily thoughts and reflections
- **Mood & Energy Logging** - Monitor your emotional and physical state
- **Insights** - View analytics and patterns in your data
- **Gratitude Log** - Practice daily gratitude journaling
- **Multiple Themes** - Customize your experience with different visual themes

## Usage

Open the application in your browser and start tracking your daily activities, moods, and thoughts. All data is stored locally in your browser.
